# AgentVault — 3-Minute Pitch Script (Consumer Reframe)

**"Your keys. Your rules. Your AI."**

---

## 1. HOOK (15 seconds)

You ask ChatGPT to plan your vacation. It offers to book the flights for you. To do that, it needs your credit card, your airline login, your passport number. You hand it all over — because you want the help. You just gave an AI the keys to your financial life. And you have no idea what it is going to do with them.

---

## 2. PROBLEM (30 seconds)

Half a billion people use ChatGPT. Apple Intelligence is launching on a billion iPhones. Alexa+ is coming to every living room. Google is putting Gemini inside Gmail. These are not chatbots anymore — they are AI assistants that touch your bank account, your email, your calendar, your medical records.

And here is the part nobody is talking about: there is no privacy layer between them and your most sensitive data. You either say yes to everything, or you say no and miss out on the magic. That is not a choice. That is a trap.

---

## 3. SOLUTION (45 seconds)

AgentVault is the consumer privacy vault for the AI era.

You store your sensitive stuff — passwords, card numbers, health info, documents — in AgentVault. When your AI assistant needs something to do its job, it does not ask you. It asks us. We issue a one-time token, scoped to exactly that task, that expires in minutes. You see every request. You approve the big ones. Everything else just works.

Think Face ID, but for your AI assistant. You do not think about it. It just keeps you safe in the background.

---

## 4. WHY NOW — FRAMEWORK ANCHOR (30 seconds)

We anchored this pitch in the **Feasibility vs. Value matrix** from Weekend 1.

On feasibility: every piece of tech we need already exists — encrypted vaults, OTP tokens, API middleware. We are not inventing anything, we are assembling proven components.

On value: 500 million people are using AI assistants today, zero consumer products solve agent permissions, and Apple, Google, and OpenAI are about to pour gasoline on this fire. High feasibility, massive value — top-right quadrant. The window is open right now, and it will not stay open.

---

## 5. BUSINESS MODEL (20 seconds)

Freemium. Free vault for up to 25 secrets. Pro at $5 a month for unlimited storage, family sharing, and premium audit logs.

And here is the expansion path: once millions of consumers are using us, we sell the enterprise version to the companies they work for. Land with consumers. Expand into the workplace.

---

## 6. DEFENSIBILITY (20 seconds)

Every interaction through AgentVault generates agent trust data — verified behavioral profiles of which AI assistants handle sensitive data responsibly and which do not. That is a data moat nobody can replicate without our scale.

Plus network effects: the more AI assistants that integrate, the more valuable we become for every user. We become the standard — the way Face ID became the standard.

---

## 7. ASK (10 seconds)

We are raising $5 million to build the consumer privacy layer for the AI era. AI assistants are already in every pocket. The permissions layer is not. We are going to fix that.

AgentVault. Your keys. Your rules. Your AI.
